# Dashboard Features

This document provides a detailed description of each feature available in the SNR.AI Demo Dashboard.

## 1. AI Command Center

The AI Command Center is a chat-based interface that serves as an intelligent system coordinator. It allows users to interact with the entire SNR.AI platform using natural language commands.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **System Management:** Perform actions like real-time workload balancing, automatic task assignment, and performance monitoring.
-   **Lead & Content Operations:** Manage AI-powered lead scoring, automated qualification, and content generation.
-   **Strategic Intelligence:** Get insights on market trends, competitor intelligence, and predictive analytics.
-   **Cross-Platform Control:** Orchestrate actions across the entire platform through event-driven automation and intelligent routing.

## 2. AI Insights

The AI Insights dashboard provides AI-powered recommendations and predictive analytics to optimize system performance.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Smart Recommendations:** Get actionable suggestions for optimizations, predictions, and warnings (e.g., rebalance team workload, prioritize a lead).
-   **Predictive Analytics:** View revenue predictions and team performance forecasts.
-   **AI Learning Progress:** Track the learning and improvement of different AI models, such as the Lead Qualification AI and Content Generation AI.
-   **Automation Opportunities:** Identify areas where automation can be increased to save time and improve efficiency.

## 3. Content Creation Bot

The Content Creation Bot is an AI-powered content generation and management system.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **AI Content Generation:** Automatically generate content for various social media platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter.
-   **Content Management:** View, edit, and manage all content in one place. Track the status (Published, Scheduled, Draft) and engagement metrics.
-   **Brand Pillar Alignment:** Ensure content aligns with your brand's core pillars (e.g., Innovation, Results).
-   **Performance Analytics:** Analyze the performance of your content on different platforms and by content type.

## 4. Feedback Loop Strategy Bot

The Feedback Loop Strategy Bot focuses on AI-powered performance optimization through continuous feedback analysis.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Feedback Processing:** Automatically process feedback from various platforms, analyze sentiment (positive, neutral, negative), and extract key insights.
-   **Performance Metrics:** Track key metrics like content engagement, conversion rates, and customer satisfaction over time.
-   **Strategy Adjustments:** Receive suggestions for strategy adjustments based on feedback analysis.
-   **AI Insights:** Get a high-level view of sentiment analysis, top feedback themes, and AI-powered recommendations.

## 5. Lead Qualification Bot

The Lead Qualification Bot is an AI-powered system for scoring and qualifying leads.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Lead Scoring:** Automatically score leads on a scale of 0-100 based on factors like budget, timeline, and company fit.
-   **Lead Categorization:** Categorize leads as "Hot," "Warm," or "Cold" based on their score.
-   **Detailed Lead View:** Get a comprehensive view of each lead, including contact information, company profile, pain points, and interests.
-   **Analytics:** Analyze lead qualification rates, conversion rates, and the performance of different lead sources.

## 6. Performance Analytics

The Performance Analytics dashboard provides a high-level overview of the entire platform's performance.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Key Metrics:** Track high-level metrics like total revenue, client satisfaction, task efficiency, and AI accuracy.
-   **Component Performance:** Monitor the performance of individual AI bots (e.g., Lead Qualifier Bot, Content Creation Bot).
-   **Client Metrics:** Get insights into client retention, average project value, and client growth.
-   **Trends & Forecasting:** Access predictive analytics and forecasting for future growth and risk indicators.

## 7. Security Center

The Security Center is a comprehensive dashboard for monitoring security and managing access control.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Security Overview:** Get an at-a-glance view of your system's security health, including SSL/TLS encryption and two-factor authentication.
-   **Access Control:** Manage user roles and permissions with role-based access control.
-   **Activity Monitoring:** Monitor all security-related events and access attempts in real-time.
-   **Compliance & Audits:** Track compliance with industry standards like GDPR and SOC 2, and view audit history.

## 8. Strategy Research Bot

The Strategy Research Bot is an AI-powered tool for business intelligence and strategic planning.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Market Research:** Generate market analysis reports, identify emerging trends, and discover new opportunities.
-   **Brand Intelligence:** Analyze your brand's health, market position, and competitive advantage.
-   **Competitor Analysis:** Get detailed analysis of your competitors, including their market share, strengths, and weaknesses.
-   **AI Insights:** Access high-level market intelligence and trend analysis to inform your strategy.

## 9. Team Status Tracker

The Team Status Tracker is a real-time system for managing workforce capacity and allocating tasks intelligently.

### Key Capabilities:
-   **Team Member Monitoring:** Track the real-time status of each team member (e.g., Active, In Meeting, Blocked), their current tasks, and their utilization.
-   **Department View:** Get an overview of each department's performance, including utilization and efficiency.
-   **Capacity Planning:** Optimize resource allocation by identifying who is available, at capacity, or overallocated.
-   **Analytics:** Analyze team performance trends, task completion rates, and compare department performance.
